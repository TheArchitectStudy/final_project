Khalya Dent

Email: khalya2110@gmail.com

Age: 16

Current School: Area Career Center University

Future School(s): Ball State University, Indiana University-Bloomington

Future Major(s): Psychology, Engineering, Computer Science

Why is it important for women to pursue STEM careers?

I think it is important for women to pursue career in STEM because the world is becoming so technologically driven. Women have always been looked at as the underdog and deserve a chance to prove themselves. Many women have so much to offer and deserve the opportunity to change the world.
