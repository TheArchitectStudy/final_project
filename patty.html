Why did you choose to work with GWC or AT&T?
I chose to work with Girls Who Code this summer to help teach and empower young females to learn software development and pursue careers in the technology field.

Why is it important for women to pursue STEM careers?
It is important for women to pursue STEM careers to create, build, and challenge their minds logically while closing the gender gap within the tech industry.
